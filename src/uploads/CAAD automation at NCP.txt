The Collaboration and Academic Activities (CAAD) department at NCP is dedicated to enhancing collaboration on both
national and international fronts. By partnering with esteemed institutions like ICTP and TWAS, NCP aims to fortify its ties
with universities, research institutes, and science academies. Through conferences, workshops, and seminars, NCP strives
to cater to the specific needs of Pakistani universities and R&D organizations while promoting science among students. The
candidate will work to develop web applications to automate various manual procedures of the CAAD department such as management of projects, MOUs, progress reports, publications, workshops/seminars/symposium/training/schoos etc.
